Data Journalism Sandbox

Welcome to the Sandbox! This is an open source project to develop blog post type content for the pharmaceutical industry.

Goal … less than 8 hours of Data Scientist LoE per initial submission
Goal … one blog post per week
30 minute meeting with policy person to prior in analysis
Strategy … end on a question, don't try to solve the world's problems in a blog post
